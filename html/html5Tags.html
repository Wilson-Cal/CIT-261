Instructions

  • Click and drag your mouse inside the canvas to draw
  • To clear the canvas, click the 'Clear' button in the menu
  • You may select different sizes from the menu's first dropdown
  • You may select different colors or an eraser from the menu's second dropdown
  • You may save your drawings by giving it a name and clicking the menu's 'Save' button
  • You may load your saved drawings by entering the drawing's name and clicking the menu's 'Load' button
